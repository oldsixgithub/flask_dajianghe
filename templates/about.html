About Us

We are a professional wireless charger manufacturer based in China, specializing in the production of wireless charging products for global B2B customers. All products are supplied directly from our factory.

Our main products include 3-in-1 wireless charging stations, MagSafe charger stands, and phone wireless chargers, widely used for wholesale, brand OEM, and distribution projects.


Factory & Production Capability

We operate as a direct factory with in-house assembly lines, warehouse management, and quality control processes.

  • Assembly production lines
  • Warehouse for raw materials and finished goods
  • Laser engraving equipment for logo customization
  • Aging and functional testing before shipment

For silk screen printing and color printing, we work with long-term cooperative factories to ensure stable quality and consistent output.


OEM & Customization Service

We provide flexible OEM and ODM services for B2B buyers. Customization options include logo engraving, housing printing, and custom packaging for bulk orders.

MOQ and pricing may vary depending on customization requirements. We are open to discussion for long-term cooperation.


Our Clients

We mainly work with:

  • Wholesale buyers
  • Brand owners
  • Distributors
  • Importers and trading companies

We do not focus on retail sales. Our goal is to support stable B2B supply with clear communication and reliable delivery.
